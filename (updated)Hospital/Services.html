Comprehensive Health Services

We provide a wide range of free medical services to ensure the well-being of every resident in Barangay Bahay Toro.

General Consultation

Primary care checkups, diagnosis, and treatment for common illnesses for adults and children.

  • Medical Clearance
  • Prescription refills
  • Referral System

Immunization

Free vaccines for infants, children, and senior citizens (Flu, Pneumonia) based on DOH schedule.

  • Routine EPI Vaccines
  • Flu & Pneumonia Shots
  • Tetanus Toxoid

Dental Services

Basic oral health care including tooth extraction, cleaning, and gum treatment.

  • Tooth Extraction
  • Oral Prophylaxis
  • Dental Education

Maternal Care

Comprehensive care for expectant mothers from pregnancy to postpartum.

  • Prenatal Checkups
  • Postnatal Care
  • Micronutrient Supplementation

Family Planning

Counseling on reproductive health and free provision of modern family planning methods.

  • Pills & Condoms
  • Injectables
  • Reproductive Counseling

TB-DOTS Clinic

Directly Observed Treatment, Short-course (DOTS) for Tuberculosis patients.

  • Sputum Microscopy
  • Free TB Medication
  • Patient Monitoring

Laboratory Services

Basic diagnostic laboratory tests to aid in medical assessment and diagnosis.

  • Urinalysis
  • Blood Sugar Screening
  • Pregnancy Test

Nutrition & Wellness

Programs designed to fight malnutrition and promote healthy lifestyles in the community.

  • Operation Timbang
  • Feeding Programs
  • Dietary Counseling

Our Facilities

Triage Area

Initial assessment and vital signs monitoring.

Barangay Pharmacy

Dispensing free basic medicines and vitamins.

Treatment Room

For minor surgeries, wound care, and nebulization.
