Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers about services in Barangay Bahay Toro, Project 8.

Appointments & Services

We are located at **Road 3, Project 8, Quezon City** (District 1). The health center is situated within the Barangay Hall compound, easily accessible to all residents of Bahay Toro.

Services are free for **bona fide residents of Barangay Bahay Toro**. You may be asked to present a valid ID with your address or a Barangay Certificate of Residency to verify your eligibility.

You can book online via our **Patient Portal** (click 'Book Now' above). Walk-ins are accepted, but priority is given to those with appointments and emergency cases.

Operating Hours & Emergency

The Health Center is open **Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM**. Selected services (like vaccination drives) may happen on Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM.

For medical emergencies, please call **911** immediately. For barangay-related assistance, you can contact the Barangay Bahay Toro Hall at **(02) 8926-8898**.

Services & Medicine

Yes, we provide free basic medicines (e.g., Paracetamol, Antibiotics, Maintenance Meds) for residents, **subject to stock availability** from the Quezon City Health Department. A valid prescription is often required.
